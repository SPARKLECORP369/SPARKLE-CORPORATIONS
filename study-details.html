BioWave™ Study — Detailed Evaluation

This page provides a detailed breakdown of how the BioWave™ stress regulation study was conducted, the student population involved, and qualitative feedback collected from participants.

Total Participants45 Students
Age Group18–24 years
Study DurationSingle Session
Primary MetricHRV

1. Student Population

The study involved 45 undergraduate students from diverse academic backgrounds. Participants reported frequent academic stress related to examinations, deadlines, and performance pressure.

2. Study Procedure

A within-subject design was used. Each participant completed the same stress-induction protocol twice — once without intervention and once with BioWave™ NeuroHaptic™ activation.

  • Timed mental arithmetic tasks
  • Memory and attention challenges
  • Countdown-based performance prompts

3. Measurement & Outcomes

  • Average HRV improvement of 22% within three minutes
  • Autonomic recovery 38% faster compared to baseline
  • Positive physiological response in most participants
