Sparkle Corporations — BioWave™ Study

Innovative Stress Regulation Through NeuroHaptic™ Technology

Objective

To scientifically measure stress reduction effect of wrist-based nerve haptic feedback combined with predictive HRV analysis.

Methodology

A controlled trial with 45 student participants performing exam-like cognitive load tasks while HRV measurements tracked stress response.

Key Result: HRV improved by 22% within 3 minutes of BioWave™ activation — faster stress recovery rate.
