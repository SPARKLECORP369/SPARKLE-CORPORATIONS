BioWave™ NeuroHaptic System

BioWave™ is a wrist-worn prototype that uses real-time Heart Rate Variability (HRV) patterns to detect rising stress and deliver subtle neurohaptic feedback aimed at accelerating autonomic recovery.

Active Regulation

Unlike conventional wearables, BioWave™ intervenes during stress rather than passively notifying users after the fact.

Student-Focused Design

Designed around academic stress scenarios such as exams, deadlines, and cognitive overload.

Research-Driven

Evaluated through an internal exploratory study using physiological biomarkers and participant feedback.

Research & Evaluation

The BioWave™ system has been evaluated through a controlled student cohort study examining physiological stress recovery and subjective calming effects.
