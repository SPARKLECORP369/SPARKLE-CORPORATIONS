BioWave™ Stress Regulation Study

This internal exploratory study evaluated the effectiveness of the BioWave™ NeuroHaptic™ system in accelerating physiological recovery from acute academic stress using Heart Rate Variability (HRV) as the primary biomarker.

Participants45
Age Range18–24
DesignWithin-Subject
HRV Gain22%

Results Overview

BioWave™ activation produced faster parasympathetic re-engagement compared to natural recovery, with the largest divergence occurring within the first three minutes post-stress.

HRV response following acute cognitive stress

BioWave™ shows accelerated autonomic recovery compared to natural baseline.
