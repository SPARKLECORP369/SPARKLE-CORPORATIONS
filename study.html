This exploratory human-factors study evaluated the BioWave™ NeuroHaptic™ system’s ability to accelerate recovery from acute academic stress. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) was used as the primary biomarker of autonomic nervous system regulation. Each participant served as their own control under baseline and BioWave™-active conditions.
