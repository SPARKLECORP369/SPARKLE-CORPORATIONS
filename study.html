BioWave™ Stress Regulation Study

This exploratory human-factors study evaluated the BioWave™ NeuroHaptic™ system’s ability to accelerate recovery from acute academic stress. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) was used as the primary biomarker of autonomic nervous system regulation.

Participants45
DesignWithin-Subject
HRV Improvement+22%
Recovery Speed+38%

BioWave™ demonstrates accelerated parasympathetic re-engagement compared to natural recovery, with the largest divergence occurring within the first three minutes post-stress.
